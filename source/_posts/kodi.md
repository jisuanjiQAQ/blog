#适用于kodiv21

进入setting→interface settings→skin将fonts由skin default 改为arial based
![设置1](/tupian/kodi/kodi1.png)

==必须修改渲染字体否则改成中文全是括号==

进入regional-language选项，选择简体中文

![设置2](/tupian/kodi/kodi2.png)
